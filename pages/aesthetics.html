Our Methodology

Four Pillars of Genetic Aesthetics

Every aesthetic protocol we design is built on four genetic categories that determine how you look, how you age, and how your body responds to treatment.

Collagen & Elastin

Your genes determine the rate at which your body produces and breaks down collagen and elastin, the structural proteins that keep skin firm, plump, and resilient. We analyze variants in COL1A1, COL3A1, and ELN genes to understand your natural production capacity, degradation rate, and UV damage susceptibility. This tells us exactly which collagen-stimulating treatments will work best for you, whether that's microneedling, PRP, or targeted peptide therapy.

18 markers analyzed

Melanin & Pigmentation

Pigmentation irregularities like melasma, sun spots, and uneven tone are driven by genetic variants in your melanin production pathways. We examine MC1R, TYR, and ASIP gene variants to understand your skin's pigmentation tendencies, photosensitivity, and predisposition to hyperpigmentation. This data guides our selection of brightening agents, laser wavelengths, and protective protocols to even your tone without overcorrecting.

14 markers analyzed

Hair Growth Genetics

Hair loss is not inevitable. Your genetic profile reveals which androgen receptor variants you carry, how sensitive your follicles are to DHT, and whether your hair loss pattern is driven by hormonal, inflammatory, or nutritional factors. We analyze AR, SRD5A2, and WNT pathway genes to determine whether PRP, low-level laser therapy, or hormonal intervention will be most effective at restoring density and slowing further loss.

22 markers analyzed

Cellular Turnover

The speed at which your skin sheds old cells and generates new ones determines how fresh, smooth, and youthful your complexion appears. We examine genes governing autophagy, keratinocyte cycling, and growth factor signaling to understand your natural renewal rate. Slow turnover means dull, rough skin; we select exfoliation methods, retinoid strengths, and regenerative treatments calibrated to your genetic baseline.

16 markers analyzed

Frequently Asked Questions

How does genetic testing improve aesthetic results?

Your genes determine how your skin produces collagen, how quickly cells turn over, and how your body responds to specific treatments. By analyzing these markers first, we can select the treatments with the highest probability of success for your specific biology, rather than using a trial-and-error approach that wastes time and money.

Is there any downtime after treatments?

Most of our treatments involve minimal to no downtime. Microneedling may cause mild redness for 24-48 hours. PRP injections may result in slight swelling at injection sites. Body contouring is completely non-invasive with zero downtime. Your clinician will provide specific aftercare instructions tailored to your treatment and skin sensitivity profile.

How soon will I see results?

Timelines vary by treatment. Skin rejuvenation clients typically see improvement within 2-3 weeks. Hair restoration results become visible at the 8-12 week mark. Body contouring results develop over 6-12 weeks. Anti-aging protocols show cumulative improvement over 60-90 days. We track progress with imaging at every milestone.

Do I need to get genetic testing before starting treatments?

While we strongly recommend genetic profiling for optimal results, it's not required to begin treatment. We can start with a clinical assessment and begin your protocol while waiting for genetic results. Once your data is in, we refine and optimize your plan. Many clients who've already completed our DNA Longevity Test have the data we need on file.

Are these treatments safe for all skin types?

Yes. Because we analyze your melanin and pigmentation genes before treatment, we can select protocols that are safe and effective for every Fitzpatrick skin type. This genetic insight is especially important for darker skin tones where certain lasers or chemical treatments carry higher risk of hyperpigmentation when applied without proper analysis.

Can I combine aesthetic treatments with other Blue Zone You services?

Absolutely, and we encourage it. Hormone optimization can dramatically improve skin quality and hair growth. IV therapy delivers collagen-building nutrients directly to your cells. Weight management programs complement body contouring results. Your clinician can coordinate a multi-service protocol that amplifies aesthetic outcomes from the inside out.
