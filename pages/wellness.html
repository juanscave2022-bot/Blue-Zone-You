Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I feel results from IV therapy?

Most clients notice improved energy and mental clarity within hours of their first IV infusion. Because nutrients are delivered directly to the bloodstream, bypassing digestive absorption, the effects are faster and more pronounced than oral supplements. Cumulative benefits build over multiple sessions.

Is hyperbaric oxygen therapy safe?

Yes. HBOT has been used clinically for decades with an excellent safety profile. Our chambers operate at 1.5-2.0 atmospheres, which is the range supported by the most clinical evidence for wellness applications. You may experience mild ear pressure during pressurization, similar to an airplane descent. Our team monitors every session.

What peptides do you use and are they legal?

We use only FDA-regulated compounding pharmacies for all peptide preparations. Common protocols include BPC-157 for tissue repair, Thymosin Alpha-1 for immune support, and CJC/Ipamorelin for growth hormone optimization. All peptides are prescribed by our licensed clinicians after a thorough medical evaluation.

How often should I come in for treatments?

Frequency depends on your protocol. IV therapy is typically weekly or bi-weekly. Red light therapy works best at 3x per week initially. HBOT protocols usually involve 10-20 sessions over 2-4 weeks. Your clinician will design a schedule that fits your life while delivering optimal results.

Can I combine multiple therapies in one visit?

Absolutely. In fact, stacking therapies is how we achieve the best results. A common combination is red light therapy followed by an IV drip, or HBOT paired with NAD+. Your clinician will design the sequencing to maximize synergy between modalities. Most combination sessions fit within 90 minutes.

Do I need genetic testing before starting wellness therapies?

It is not required, but we strongly recommend it. Genetic testing allows us to customize your IV formulations based on how your body actually absorbs and processes specific nutrients. Without it, we use standard evidence-based protocols. With it, every drip is precision-matched to your biology.
