About Blue Zone You

Founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to the science of living longer. We combine genetic testing, precision medicine, and the lifestyle principles of the world's Blue Zones to help you add healthy, vibrant years to your life.

Blue Zones are five regions where people consistently live past 100: Okinawa, Sardinia, Nicoya, Ikaria, and Loma Linda. We took those principles and combined them with cutting-edge genomics to create personalized longevity protocols built around your unique biology.
