Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from a regular weight loss clinic?
Am I a candidate for GLP-1 therapy?

Candidacy is determined through a clinical evaluation that includes metabolic labs, your genetic predisposition to GLP-1 response, BMI, health history, and treatment goals. Not everyone is a fit, and that's by design. We only prescribe GLP-1 medications when the clinical data supports it and when it's part of a comprehensive protocol that protects muscle mass and long-term metabolic health.

Will I regain the weight if I stop GLP-1 medication?

This is exactly why we pair GLP-1 therapy with metabolic, nutritional, and exercise protocols. The medication creates a window of accelerated progress, while the lifestyle and metabolic interventions build the foundation for maintaining results long-term. We design tapering protocols and transition plans so that when you're ready to come off medication, your habits and metabolism can sustain your new body composition.

How quickly will I see results?
Do I need genetic testing to join a weight program?

Genetic testing is included in our metabolic weight loss program because it gives us the most precise data to work with. If you've already completed genetic testing with Blue Zone You, we'll use your existing results. The testing ensures every element of your protocol is matched to your biology rather than based on population averages.

Can I combine weight management with other Blue Zone You services?

Absolutely. Many of our weight management clients also work with our hormone optimization and wellness teams. Hormones like testosterone and thyroid directly impact metabolism, and services like IV therapy and peptides can accelerate recovery and fat loss. Your clinician will recommend complementary services based on your genetic and metabolic data.
