Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from 23andMe or AncestryDNA?

Consumer DNA tests focus on ancestry and basic trait information. Our panels are medical-grade, analyzing 200+ markers specifically linked to longevity, disease risk, and treatment response. Your results come with a clinician consultation and a personalized protocol, not just a report.

Is the cheek swab really painless?

Yes. You simply swab the inside of your cheek for about 30 seconds. There's no blood draw, no needles, and no fasting required. The at-home kit includes detailed instructions and a prepaid return envelope.

How long until I get my results?

Results are typically available within 2-3 weeks from the time your sample reaches the lab. We'll notify you as soon as your Longevity Blueprint is ready and schedule your one-on-one clinician review at a time that works for you.

Do I need to take the test more than once?

Your DNA doesn't change, so the core genetic test only needs to be done once. However, we recommend retesting your epigenetic age every 6 months to track how your biological age is responding to your protocol. This is how we measure real progress.

Is my genetic data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your genetic data is protected under HIPAA regulations and is never shared with third parties, insurance companies, or data brokers. We use CLIA-certified labs with enterprise-grade security. You own your data.

Can I combine genetic testing with other Blue Zone You services?

That's exactly how we recommend using it. Your genetic test results inform every other service we offer, from hormone optimization to IV therapy to weight management. The more your clinician knows about your genetics, the more precisely they can tailor every protocol.
