The Science of NAD+

NAD+ is a coenzyme found in every living cell.

Energy Production

NAD+ is required for mitochondrial function. Without adequate NAD+, your cells cannot produce ATP efficiently, leading to fatigue and metabolic dysfunction.

DNA Repair

NAD+ activates sirtuins and PARPs, the enzymes responsible for repairing damaged DNA. This is one of the primary mechanisms of biological aging.

Neuroprotection

NAD+ supports neurotransmitter production and protects neurons from oxidative damage. Clients report dramatic improvements in mental clarity and focus.
